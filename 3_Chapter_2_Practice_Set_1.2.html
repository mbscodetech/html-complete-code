WHAT IS NATURE?

 Our forests, rivers, oceans and soils provide 
    us with the food we eat, the air we breathe, the water 
    we irrigate our crops with. We also rely on them for 
    numerous other goods and services we depend on for
     our health, happiness and prosperity.
